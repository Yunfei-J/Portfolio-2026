# Prototypes

# Enter lobby and join a room

Users can join or create a room to start the game.

Rooms can be filtered by language preference, genre preference, and room size.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63a0d533a7afeac89489815e/670ac6d9e0143bf29f7beafc_Screen%20Shot%202024-10-12%20at%202.54.42%20PM.png)

# Teams take turns to ask, answer, and vote, with the goal of finding the hidden non-human player

Each team is made up of one AI player (bot) and three real players.

The teams take turns asking the opposing team a question.

Based on the responses given, the objective is to figure out which answer came from the AI and which came from the human players.
